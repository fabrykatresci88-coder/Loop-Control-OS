# Business Module

## Market Analysis
- **Target Audience:** Dental clinics, independent dentists, dental hygienists, orthodontists.
- **Market Size:** Growing demand for digital solutions in healthcare, specifically dental practices adopting CRM tools to improve patient management and retention.
- **Trends:** Increasing use of tele-dentistry, patient engagement platforms, and integration with dental imaging and billing software.
- **Pain Points:** Inefficient patient scheduling, lack of personalized communication, difficulty tracking patient history and treatment plans.

## Competitor Analysis
- **Direct Competitors:**
  - Dentrix Ascend: Comprehensive dental practice management with CRM features.
  - Curve Dental: Cloud-based dental software with patient communication tools.
  - Lighthouse 360: Focused on automated patient communication and recall.
- **Indirect Competitors:**
  - General CRM platforms adapted for dental use (e.g., Salesforce Health Cloud).
  - Appointment scheduling apps with limited CRM capabilities.
- **Competitive Advantages to Target:**
  - User-friendly interface tailored specifically for dental workflows.
  - Seamless integration with dental imaging and billing systems.
  - Affordable pricing for small to medium practices.
  - Strong focus on patient engagement and retention.

## Pricing Strategy
- **Pricing Model:** Subscription-based SaaS with tiered plans.
- **Suggested Tiers:**
  - Basic: Core CRM features, appointment scheduling, patient database management.
  - Professional: Adds automated reminders, marketing campaigns, analytics.
  - Enterprise: Full integration with dental imaging, billing, and advanced reporting.
- **Price Points:** 
  - Basic: $30/user/month
  - Professional: $60/user/month
  - Enterprise: Custom pricing based on integration needs.
- **Additional Revenue Streams:**
  - Add-ons for tele-dentistry modules.
  - Premium support and training packages.
  - Data migration and customization services.

## Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
- **Core Features:**
  - Patient database with treatment history.
  - Appointment scheduling and calendar management.
  - Automated appointment reminders via SMS/email.
  - Basic reporting on patient visits and treatments.
- **User Roles:**
  - Dentist
  - Receptionist
  - Office Manager
- **Integration:**
  - Basic integration with popular dental billing software.
- **Goals:**
  - Validate market demand.
  - Gather user feedback for feature prioritization.
  - Establish initial customer base with scalable architecture.
