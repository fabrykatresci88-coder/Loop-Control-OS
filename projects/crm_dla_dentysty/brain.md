# Brain Module

## Problem Framing
Dentists often struggle with managing patient relationships efficiently due to fragmented communication, appointment scheduling conflicts, and lack of personalized patient data insights. Existing CRM solutions are either too generic or overly complex, leading to underutilization and reduced patient satisfaction.

## Solution Scope
The Brain module will serve as the intelligent core of the CRM, integrating patient data, appointment history, treatment plans, and communication logs. It will leverage AI-driven analytics to provide actionable insights, automate routine tasks (e.g., reminders, follow-ups), and enable personalized patient engagement strategies tailored to dental practices.

## Core Value
- Centralized intelligence hub that simplifies decision-making for dentists.
- Enhances patient retention through personalized, timely interactions.
- Reduces administrative burden by automating repetitive tasks.
- Improves treatment outcomes by providing data-driven recommendations.
